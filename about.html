About

The championship is played in F1 22, one of the most realistic and immersive racing games available. The virtual tracks are faithfully recreated, and the cars are modelled to behave just like their real-world counterparts. This means that the races are incredibly competitive and require skill, strategy, and nerves of steel to win.

The races in the Gulag Formula 1 Championship 2023 are designed to be exciting and challenging, and follow a specific format. Firstly, the championship uses a 15-minute qualifying round to determine the starting grid for the race. During this time, drivers take to the track and try to set the fastest lap time possible. The drivers who post the fastest times will start at the front of the grid, giving them a significant advantage over their competitors.

Once the qualifying round is over, the drivers then take to the track for the race itself. The races in the Gulag Formula 1 Championship are set to 25% of the complete race distance, which means that they're shorter than a full race but still long enough to be challenging.

During the race, drivers have to navigate the track, manage their tires and fuel, and try to overtake their opponents. The championship features realistic damage and penalties, so drivers need to be careful not to damage their cars or break the rules, or they risk being penalized and losing valuable time.

The Gulag Formula 1 Championship is not just about winning, though. It's also about the community. Drivers come together every Sunday to compete, chat, and have fun. The championship is open to everyone, regardless of skill level, and drivers are encouraged to help each other improve.
